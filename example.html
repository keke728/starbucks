Introduction

In this final project, you will design an interactive data experience that allows users to explore and uncover a story. Your project should be a single, self-contained interactive experience, in the form of either a one-page interactive story or an interactive dashboard.

Interaction Requirement

Include at least one interaction

Your interaction should change what users see, understand, or discover. (e.g., filtering or selecting data to reveal patterns, comparing groups or perspectives, navigating across views or moments in time, or inputting values to explore “what-if” scenarios.)
