Introduction

Starbucks drinks can look similar at first glance, but their calories, sugar, and caffeine can tell very different stories. Some are simple coffee orders. Others are closer to dessert in a cup.

This interactive story uses Starbucks nutrition data to reveal which drinks are strongest, sweetest, and heavier than they seem.
